INGREDIENTS

  • Clove Garlic
  • 1 lemon (squeeze for juice)
  • 16 oz Tahini
  • Salt
  • 1 cup ice water
  • 30 oz Chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
  • 1/4 pine nuts
  • high smoking temperature oil such as canola or grapesed

DIRECTIONS

  1. Pre heat over 350 degrees
  2. Tahini: Drop garlic, lemon juice, tahini, pinch of salt, and cumin into food processor for 1 minute then slowly stream in ice water
  3. Hummus: Add chickpeas to food processor till complety blended ~ 3 minutes
  4. Toast pine nuts with sprinkling of oil and salt
